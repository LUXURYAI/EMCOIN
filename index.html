You can buy USDT inside Bitget Wallet or send USDT from another exchange to your Bitget Wallet.

Swap USDT to EMCOIN

For Messenger activation, use only the official payment wallet published on this website.

Always check the network, contract address, amount and fee before confirming any transaction.
